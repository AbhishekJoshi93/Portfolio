Book My Trip

Frontend & Backend Website

Tools

Html, Css, Js, Jquery, FirebaseAuthentication, FirebaseDatabase, FirebaseHosting

Description

Book My Trip is a booking website for traveller and completely running on firebase. First of all user are authenticate with firebaseauth and then they can book their flights and hotels of their choice. Also at the company side all bookings and feedbacks are shown and then they can confirm the booking manually.
